## Mechanism of Action
5-HT3 antagonist
## Indications
[[Post-operative Nausea and Vomiting]]
NNT 6 for postoperative nausea and 7 for postoperative vomiting
## Dosage
4 mg IV or 8mg PO disolving (50% bioavailable) [^1]
## Complications
NNH 36 for headache
NNH 31 for elevated LFTs
NNH 23 for constipation
