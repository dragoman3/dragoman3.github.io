## Pharmacology
Half life is 40 hours
## Indications
[[Post-operative Nausea and Vomiting]]
- reduces incidence of POV (not nausea)
- More efficacious than [[Ondansetron]][^1]
- Useful as a *prophylactic anti-emetic*
## Dosage
40, 80, 125 mg PO are all effective
